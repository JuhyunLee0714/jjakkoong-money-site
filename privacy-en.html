JjakkoongMoney Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026 | Version: 3.0

JjakkoongMoney (hereinafter "Company") values the personal information of its users and complies with the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) and other applicable laws of the Republic of Korea.

1. Personal Information Collected

[Required Information]

  • Email address: Registration, login, account recovery
  • Password (encrypted): Authentication
  • Nickname/display name: Partner identification

[Social Login Information]

  • Google: Email, profile name, profile photo
  • Kakao: Email, nickname, profile photo
  • Naver: Email, nickname, profile photo

[Information Generated Through Service Use]

  • Income/expense transactions, budgets, asset/liability information
  • Gift money records, decision voting history
  • Partner connection info, tags, category settings
  • Service usage records, access logs

[Advertising Information]

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID): Personalized ads via Google AdMob
  • App usage statistics: Ad performance measurement

[Crash & Diagnostics Information]

  • Crash logs (stack traces), device model, OS version, app version, memory usage: App stability monitoring via Firebase Crashlytics

[AI Analysis Information]

  • Aggregated financial data (category spending amounts/ratios, income/expense totals, savings rate, budget usage): AI report and insight generation via Anthropic Claude API
  • Note: Data sent for AI analysis does not include personally identifiable information and is transmitted in aggregated, anonymized form.

2. Purpose of Use

  • Registration, management, and authentication
  • Budget data storage, synchronization, and service provision
  • Data sharing between partners (Couple Mode)
  • Budget management, report generation, statistical analysis
  • AI weekly/monthly report and insight generation
  • Displaying advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Service quality improvement and error analysis

3. Retention and Destruction

  • Account and transaction data: Until account deletion
  • Access logs: 3 months
  • Service usage records: 3 years
  • All data permanently deleted upon account deletion in an unrecoverable manner

[Legally Required Retention Periods]

  • Records of contracts or withdrawal: 5 years (E-Commerce Act)
  • Records of payment and goods supply: 5 years (E-Commerce Act)
  • Records of consumer complaints or disputes: 3 years (E-Commerce Act)
  • Records of advertising: 6 months (E-Commerce Act)
  • Communication fact verification data: 3 months (Telecommunications Privacy Act)

4. Third-Party Disclosure

The Company does not provide personal information to third parties without user consent. Exceptions: Lawful requests from investigative authorities, prior user consent.

[Third-Party Provision Details]

  • Google LLC (AdMob): Ad identifiers, app usage stats - Ad services
  • Google LLC (Vision API): Receipt images - OCR text recognition (immediately discarded)
  • Google LLC (Firebase Crashlytics): Crash logs, device info - App stability monitoring
  • RevenueCat Inc.: User ID, subscription status - Subscription management
  • Anthropic PBC (Claude API): Aggregated financial data - AI report/insight generation (immediately discarded, no PII transmitted)

5. Data Processing Delegation

  • Supabase Inc. (USA): Database hosting, authentication services
  • Google LLC (USA): Advertising (AdMob), OCR (Vision API), Error analysis (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • RevenueCat Inc. (USA): Subscription payment management
  • Anthropic PBC (USA): AI analysis service (weekly/monthly reports, insight generation)

6. Security Measures

  • Transmission encryption: TLS/HTTPS for all data transfers
  • Storage encryption: Passwords hashed with bcrypt
  • Access control: Data isolation through Row Level Security (RLS)
  • Token security: JWT tokens stored in device secure storage (Keychain/Keystore)

7. User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • View, correct, or delete personal information
  • Request suspension of data processing
  • Withdraw consent (Settings > Delete Account)
  • Export data (CSV format)
  • Contact: In-app settings or jjakkoongmoney@gmail.com

8. Advertising and Tracking

  • iOS: Tracking permission requested per App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.
  • Declining tracking does not restrict service usage.
  • Disable ad personalization: iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking, Android Settings > Google > Ads

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 14. If we learn that information from a child under 14 has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

  • Anthropic PBC (USA): Aggregated financial data (AI analysis)
